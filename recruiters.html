Past Recruiters

The SOET has previously worked with a number of international and national companies and institution.
Our students have worked in these institution for training and research purposes.

Some of the institutions we have worked with:

  1. Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
  2. Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  3. WIPRO.
  4. National Institute of Technical Teachers Training and Research (NITTTR), Chandigarh.
  5. National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELET), Srinagar.
  6. Sher-e-Kashmir Institute of Medical Science (SKIMS), Srinagar.
  7. Jammu and Kashmir Bank.
