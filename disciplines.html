Disciplines

 The department is presently offering a B. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) which is a 4 year degree programme. Striving hard for excellence, the Department plans to diversify and expand its activities in the years to come .The programme is aimed at fulfilling the ever increasing demand of trained computer science professionals in the industry. The students in this course receive rigorous training to face the challenges of today’s competitive world. The course is updated from time to time to keep up with the ever evolving IT sector. Emphasis is laid not only on the theoretical concepts but also on practical experience and industry interaction.
The Department is presently also offering a 2 year Post-Graduate programme leading to the degree of M. Tech. in Information Technology which has replaced the previous M.Sc IT programme.

Curriculum

First Year

  • Programming in C.
  • Physics I & II.
  • Chemistry I & II.
  • Engineering Drawing.
  • Engineering Workshop.
  • Mathematics I & II.
  • Sociology.
  • Communication skills.
  • Bacis Electricals.
  • Engineering Mechanics.

Second Year

  • Data Structures with C.
  • Operating System.
  • Object Oriented programming (C++).
  • Discreet Mathemtics.
  • Digital Logic.
  • Mathematics III & IV.
  • Basic Electronics.
  • Environmental Studies.
  • Economis for Engineers.

Third Year

  • Java Programming.
  • Database Management System.
  • System Analysis and Design.
  • Computer Organization and Architecture .
  • Computer Networks.
  • Software Engineering.
  • Design of Algorithms.
  • Theory of Computation.

Fourth Year

  • Compiler Design.
  • Microprocessors.
  • Web Design and Technology.
