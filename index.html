About the department

The Department of Information Technology is a flagship department under the SOET. Having an up-to-date curriculum, well trained faculty and an excellent infrastructure, the department is committed to develop highly skilled Information Technology (IT) professionals.

Students

CUK draws on a reserve of extraordinary talent as a by-product of its selective, stringent and consequently, amazingly successful Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) for admissions to all the UG programmes.



Disciplines

The Department is presently offering a B. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) which is a 4 year degree programme. Striving hard for excellence, the Department plans to diversify and expand its activities in the years to come.
