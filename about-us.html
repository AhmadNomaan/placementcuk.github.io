About the Placement Cell

Placement and training cell is a facilitation unit which is responsible for interacting with various industrial, management and research organisations in the country and abroad with the dual aim of ensuring that the students are given adequate technical exposure / industrial training from 2nd year onwards and also enabling them to get employment in organisations whose functional requirements of high calibre engineers, scientists and managers are best met by graduate and post-graduate students of the Institute.

The major functions of this unit are:

  • To invite industries and organisations of repute with the aim of providing employment to students and assisting them in the placement process on CUK campus.
  • To organise lectures and workshops for students to provide supplemental information on career counselling, interview techniques, soft skills development and introduction to industrial sectors / the professional world.
