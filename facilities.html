Facilities

Data Center

The SOET houses a data center that is dedicated
to the technical needs of the institution.
The data center provides a learning opportunity to expose students to latest technologies.

Computer Center

The School of Engineering and Technology has dedicated computer labs for undergraduate and research scholars.
The labs are well equipped with top of the line hardware.

Physics Laboratory

The School of Engineering and Technology houses a physics laboratory for developing a scientific temper in
students. The lab has equiment to handle advance level experiments.

Electronics Laboratory

The Electronics lab is a new addition to the School of Engineering and Technology.
It has equipment aimed at satisfing the scientific curiosity of students.

Library

The Central Library of the university is host to a huge amount of books with MOU's with other libraries all across the world. The Univerity librariy has access to journals from institutes such as Institute of Physics, UK.
