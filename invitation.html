Invitation

Sir/Madam,

Subject: Invitation to visit our department for Campus Recruitment of 2021 passing out batch.

It gives us immense pleasure to extend you and your team a most cordial invitation to participate in the Campus Recruitment of our Institute for the 2021 passing out batch in the situation of this pandemic, where all the students of not only our country but students from over the globe have suffered a tremendous loss in terms of education, studies and other co-curricular activities. Now, more than ever, the emphasis is on Institute-Industry interaction and both the Institute, organizing the Campus Recruitment Drive, and the Industry, participating in the same, find it mutually beneficial. We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the technical institutes in the northern part of our country and our university has almost completed eleven years since its establishment by the Govt. of Jammu &Kashmir in 2009. At present, there are one B.Tech. courses running for the branch Computer Science and Engineering. Moreover, this would be our first batch in this field to pass out in this course.

Post Graduate courses are conducted for the course of M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering .Within the short tenure of eleven years; our university has been able to establish the reputation for its students within the state of Jammu &Kashmir and outside the state, as well as in other countries in their respective disciplines. In the awake of the liberalization of economy, CUK as an institution contributed something great in the revolution of technical education in the state and assured total quality education. The institution maintained and manufactured the technocrats to the needs of industries. The campus does have relationship status with several major institution in creating Mind Share and Human Capital to cater the needs of industry. In the subject prospective, it may be mentioned here that, we have the talent pool of B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE). The resources are willing to locate anywhere within the country or overseas & they have all the qualities to be a part of your family. It will be of high appreciation if you visit our campus either in person (if feasible) or in online mode in these circumstances of the prevailing pandemic (COVID-19).

We appeal to your good self to please pay a visit to our institute as per your earliest convenience or organise an online drive for placement (whatever, feasible) and discover the treasure of potential engineers, and recruit the best students of our 2021 passing out batch. Looking forward to your valued cooperation and a long-term association. We are expecting that proposal will peruse your due favour.

With regards

Er. Zahoor Ahmad Najar
